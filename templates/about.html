About Employee Management System

Efficiently manage your workforce with ease

The Employee Management System is a web-based application designed to help organizations manage employee data efficiently and securely.

This system replaces manual record keeping with a digital solution where administrators can add, update, view, and delete employee details seamlessly.

It also includes features such as secure login, OTP-based password recovery, and structured employee data management.
